# Brand Positioning And ICP

## Positioning

`لاميس للجمال` is a premium Saudi-focused beauty and wellness brand for women who want to feel polished, feminine, fresh, and confident without complicated routines.

The store should feel like a real DTC brand with a curated product line, not a generic dropshipping store. It must communicate:

- A branded product system under one beauty philosophy.
- Saudi-local understanding: language, delivery, COD, WhatsApp support, climate, occasions, and daily routines.
- Premium value through education, proof, reviews, routines, guarantees, and elevated design.
- Beauty from inside and outside: collagen, hair support, and freshness/body odor confidence.

Do not claim ownership, exclusivity, certifications, SFDA approval, lab testing, or clinical proof unless those assets are real and available. The site can say "منتقاة بعناية من لاميس للجمال" and "ضمن روتين لاميس" until private label or exclusive rights exist.

## ICP

Primary customer:

- Saudi women, age 23-42.
- Mobile-first shoppers from Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam, Khobar, Makkah, Madinah, and major cities.
- Active on Snapchat and TikTok.
- Buys beauty, fragrance, supplements, hair care, abayas, skincare, and wellness products online.
- Wants visible self-care results, but is skeptical of exaggerated claims.
- Trusts Saudi dialect, Arabic reviews, clear COD, fast delivery, WhatsApp support, and proof shown close to CTAs.
- Responds to premium packaging, before/after style UGC, influencer tone, ingredient education, and "routine" framing.

Secondary customer:

- Women buying gifts or routine bundles.
- Women who already tried one product and can be moved to a second/third product through cart cross-sell and timed upsell.

## Emotional Drivers

Use copy that speaks to identity and daily feelings:

- "أبغى أحس أنوثتي باينة بدون مجهود."
- "أبغى أطلع وأنا واثقة من شعري وبشرتي وريحتي."
- "أبغى روتين بسيط يناسب يومي، مو عشر خطوات."
- "أبغى منتج مرتب وواضح وأحس أنه يستاهل سعره."

## Pain Points

Collagen latte:

- Fine lines, tired skin, dullness, lack of glow, looking older in photos, wanting a daily beauty ritual.

Rosemary and biotin spray:

- Hair fall after stress, postpartum, styling, heat, hijab humidity, thin-looking hairline, weak roots.

Sugar-free chlorophyll gummies:

- Sweat odor anxiety, social embarrassment, summer/heat, long workdays, university, gatherings, wanting freshness from inside.

## Trust Signals For Saudi Women

Show these above the fold and near checkout:

- الدفع عند الاستلام.
- شحن داخل السعودية.
- دعم واتساب بالعربي.
- تقييمات عميلات سعوديات موثقة.
- ضمان استبدال أو تعويض واضح.
- مكونات واضحة وطريقة استخدام سهلة.
- صور/فيديو UGC حقيقية.
- ختم "روتين مختار من لاميس".
- "لا نعرض أي شهادة أو اعتماد إلا إذا كان موثق ومتوفر للعميلة."

## Authority And Proof System

The site needs a proof layer, not fake proof:

- Ingredient proof: explain each active ingredient in simple Arabic.
- Routine proof: show where product fits in daily routine.
- Social proof: verified review component with city, age range, product, date, and optional video.
- Usage proof: photos and demo videos from real UGC.
- Quality proof: supplier documents, COA, batch info, SFDA/import docs only if actually available.
- Policy proof: clear COD, delivery, returns, privacy, and support commitments.

## Brand Voice

Arabic voice:

- Saudi feminine, warm, premium, direct.
- Avoid exaggerated medical language.
- Use "يساعد", "يدعم", "مصمم لروتينك", "إحساس", "مظهر", "انتعاش".
- Avoid "يعالج", "يشفي", "مضمون 100%", "نتائج طبية", "معتمد" unless legally verified.

Example tone:

```text
روتين صغير يغيّر إحساسك بنفسك.
لاميس تجمع لك منتجات جمال وعناية مختارة بعناية، بتجربة شراء واضحة، دفع عند الاستلام، ودعم عربي قريب منك.
```

## Compliance Guardrails

Because the products touch beauty, supplements, and wellness:

- Do not claim to treat diseases, hormonal problems, alopecia, medical hair loss, hyperhidrosis, or skin disease.
- Do not guarantee anti-aging results. Use appearance-focused language.
- Add a gentle disclaimer on supplement-like products: "مكمل/منتج عناية لا يغني عن استشارة مختص، والنتائج تختلف من شخص لآخر."
- If selling ingestible products in KSA, verify regulatory requirements, labeling, import, and SFDA status before launch.
