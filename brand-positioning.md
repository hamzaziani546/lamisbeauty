**لاميس | Lamis**
(dropped "للجمال" / "Beauty" — the brand stands alone, like a pharmacy name)

**Tagline:** مكملات جمال بمعايير صيدلانية
**English:** Beauty supplements, pharmaceutical standards.

## Brand Identity

**Archetype:** The Trusted Expert (Pharmacist × Premium Feminine)

Lamis is the knowledgeable friend who also happens to be a pharmacist. She doesn't sell hype — she recommends what works, explains why, and stands behind it. She speaks clear Arabic with Moroccan warmth, understands local climate and COD culture, and never talks down to you.

**Voice:** Confident, clinical-warm, specific (numbers over adjectives), Saudi dialect, zero fluff.

**Tone pillars:**
1. **علمي بدون تعقيد** — Scientific without being boring. Use real dosages, cite mechanisms, but explain in everyday Saudi Arabic.
2. **ثقة مو غرور** — Confident, not arrogant. Let the SFDA certification, dosages, and reviews speak.
3. **صدق مطلق** — Radical honesty. "Results in 4–8 weeks" not "instant glow". Real disclaimers, real expectations.
4. **سعودية ١٠٠٪** — COD, local shipping, WhatsApp support in Arabic, formulated for Saudi heat.

## Brand Positioning

**Category:** Premium Nutraceutical Gummies for Moroccan Women
**Position:** صيدلية جمالك الخاصة — Your personal beauty pharmacy.

**Positioning Statement:**
For Moroccan women (25–40) who are tired of overpromising beauty products and confusing supplement options, Lamis is the trusted nutraceutical brand that delivers science-backed gummy supplements with pharmaceutical-grade transparency — clear dosages, quality-controlled batches, and a 30-day golden guarantee. Sold with COD, fast delivery (same-day Casablanca, 1–2 days nationwide), WhatsApp confirmation, prices in MAD.

**Competitive Moat:**

- Research-dose transparency (20mg Lutein, 100mg Chlorophyll, Type I Marine Collagen)
- Saudi-first experience (COD, local shipping, Arabic WhatsApp support)
- 30-day money-back guarantee (risk reversal)

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

**Who:** Saudi women, 25–40 years old
**Where:** Major cities (Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam, Khobar) + all KSA
**Pain points:**
- Dark circles that don't go away with concealer
- Dull skin, hair loss, brittle nails
- Body odor embarrassment in Saudi heat + long work hours
- Overwhelmed by too many products and confusing supplement options
- Distrust of imported supplements with no local certification

**What they want:**
- Simple routine (not 10 steps)
- Tasty (gummies, not pills)
- Results they can see (4–8 weeks)
- Trust (SFDA, real dosages, money-back guarantee)
- Saudi shopping experience (COD, Arabic support, fast local shipping)

## Design Principles

1. **Clinical clean** — White space, sharp typography, minimal decoration. Think pharmacy shelf, not Instagram boutique.
2. **Badge-heavy** — ONSSA certification, dosage numbers, and guarantee badges visible on every scroll.
3. **Proof-first** — Reviews, numbers, and science before emotional copy.
4. **Saudi-native** — RTL-first, KSA dialect, local city names, COD-prominent.

## Offer Architecture & CRO Strategy (AOV / Confirmation / Delivery)

### The Pack Math
Every product = **60 gummies = 30 days supply** at 2 gummies/day.
Each box is a full month, not a "trial size".

### Tiered Offer Structure (per product)

| Tier | Boxes | Duration       | Price   | Per Box | Per Day  | Save     | Position           |
|------|-------|----------------|---------|---------|----------|----------|--------------------|
| 1    | 1     | شهر كامل       | 199 SAR | 199     | ≈7 SAR   | —        | Trial entry        |
| 2    | 2     | شهرين          | 349 SAR | 175     | ≈6 SAR   | 49 SAR   | Continuity         |
| 3    | 3     | 3 شهور         | 449 SAR | 150     | ≈5 SAR   | 148 SAR  | **Auto-selected** ⭐ |

### Why this beats single-piece pricing for COD
- **Higher AOV**: pre-selecting 3-month tier raises average ticket from ~199 to ~449.
- **Higher confirmation rate**: customer picking 3 months has higher intent → confirms at WhatsApp call.
- **Higher delivery rate**: customer mentally committed to a 3-month routine is less likely to refuse package at the door.
- **Lower per-delivery cost as % of revenue**: same shipping cost on a 449 SAR order vs 199 SAR.

### Cross-sell Bundle (any 2 products together)
- Price: 349 SAR (vs 398 separately) — save 49 SAR
- Triggered on PDP after main offer commitment
- Shipping consolidates → improves margin

### Removed Trust-Killers
1. "بيانات تجريبية - يُستبدل بتقييمات حقيقية عند الإطلاق" → REMOVED (signals fake data)
2. "صور المنتجات الأصلية تُضاف قريباً" → Replaced with "60 علكة · شهر كامل"
3. "محتوى UGC يُضاف قريباً" + 6 empty placeholders → Replaced with **3-step delivery reassurance section**
4. "صورة المكونات / صورة الاستخدام / صورة المختبر" → Replaced with informational badges
5. "(+10,000) عميلة" + "+10,000 علبة بيعت" → Removed (inflated, untrusted) → Replaced with verifiable claims (60 علكة، 30 يوم، SFDA)
6. Pulsing "الكمية محدودة" badge → Removed (felt manipulative) → Replaced with informational duration badge

### Added Confirmation/Delivery-rate Boosters
1. **Value stacking section** ("60 علكة + 30 يوم + 2 يومياً + 7 ريال/اليوم") right after hero — anchors high perceived value before checkout.
2. **3-step Order Experience section** — explains WhatsApp confirmation flow, shipping timeline, and "تستلمين وتدفعين" reassurance.
3. **Per-day cost display** on every offer (≈ 7 / 6 / 5 ريال/اليوم) — reframes price into trivial daily commitment.
4. **Pharmacy comparison anchor** — "lo شريتي من الصيدلية يكلفك أكثر من 400 ريال شهرياً".
5. **FAQ entry on confirmation flow** — addresses "كيف يتم تأكيد الطلب؟" head-on.
6. **Duration framing on every offer** — "شهر كامل / شهرين / 3 شهور" instead of "1 / 2 / 3 قطع".

### Confirmation/Delivery Rate Targets
- Confirmation rate: 70–80% (target after these changes)
- Delivery rate: 75–85% (target)
- Net delivery: 55–65%
- AOV target: 380+ SAR (vs current ~280 with old pricing)
